## About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at LUISS Guido Carli University, where I am affiliated with the CESARE Laboratory. I obtained my PhD in Economics from LUISS in 2024.

My research focuses on **experimental and behavioral economics**, with a particular interest in how individuals process risk and information.

Currently, I am investigating the interaction between **artificial intelligence** and ideological bias in information processing.

- **MSc in Economics**, LUISS Guido Carli University / Einaudi Institute, 2019
