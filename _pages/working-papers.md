## Working Papers

### Artificial Intelligence, Misinformation, and Ideological Bias: Experimental Evidence

This paper examines how AI-generated content and AI-based detection signals affect information processing and ideological bias. Using an incentivized laboratory experiment with three phases, I find that neither AI-generated misinformation nor the introduction of AI-based detection significantly improves overall classification accuracy. However, making AI explicit increases skepticism toward information. Classification errors are strongly driven by congruence bias, and the corrective effect of the AI signal is substantially attenuated when congruence bias is high.

**Status:** Working Paper
